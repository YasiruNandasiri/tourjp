Places to Visit:


Nara Temple & Park

Todaiji (Great Eastern Temple) is one of Japan's most famous and historically significant temples and a landmark of Nara. It has been used as a location in several Japanese films and television dramas. so, it is one of the places you absolutely cannot miss visiting in Nara.


Kiyomizudera Temple

Kiyomizudera (Pure Water Temple) is one of the most celebrated temples of Japan. It is located in Kyoto and the temple is part of the Historic Monuments of Ancient Kyoto. During cherry blossom season, autumn and other special occasions, you can enter the temple after dark to enjoy the trees and buildings of the temple expertly lit-up to showcase their beauty.


Arashiyama Bamboo Forest

The Arashiyama Bamboo Grove is located on the western outskirts of Japan's ancient capital of Kyoto. It is a Unesco World Heritage Site and one of the Kyoto's most iconic sights. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove is considered one of the most photographed places in Kyoto. Best thing is it is open 24/7 and admission is free.


Kobe City

Kobe is Japan's seventh-largest city and the third-largest port city after Tokyo and Yokohama. Kobe Harborland, Meriken Park and Mount Rokko are some of the most attractive tourist destinations in Kobe city. Also you can enjoy the views of the port town Kobe on the Kobe Bay Cruise


Osaka City Tour

Osaka is Japan's second largest metropolitan area after Tokyo. There are a lot of places to visit in Osaka such as Osaka castle the symbol of Osaka and a real cultural and historical landmark in the city, Abeno Harukas: the tallest building in Osaka, If you’re a real foodie, Dotonbori should be on the top of your list of places to visit in Osaka. If you’re traveling with kids, you should definitely spend a day or two at Universal Studios Japan.


Things To Do:


Flowers Sightseeing

Japan’s most celebrated flower is the Cherry blossom (Sakura flower). However, that only takes place in March and April. The few days when the Cherry Blossom peaks are the busiest and most beautiful time of the year. The beginning of summer is the best time to see hydrangeas. This plant is associated with the rainy season in Japan, so if you see it, the best time to see them is from mid-June to mid-July. You can see many more flowers like Tulips, Wisteria, Nemophila etc During seasons.


Fruit Picking

You can enjoy fruit picking while travel in japan. Enjoying Grapes picking during August to September, Strawberry picking during December to May, Apple picking during late September to early December.
